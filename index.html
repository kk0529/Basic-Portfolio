About Me

My name is Kiran. I am a graduate of Binghamton University with a bachelors degree in Biology. This realization has led me to begin coding in my free time. Shortly after messing around with basic code, I decided that this was something I could do as a career that would FULFILL ME. I decided that the best way to push for that passion was to leave my job and attend a coding bootcamp in order to completely immerse myself in the art of web development.
